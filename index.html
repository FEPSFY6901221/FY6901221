Abstract

This article is an analysis for students and academic staff of the advantages and disadvantages of the use of generative AI
 such as ChatGPT for university assignments. It has been known that in the last few years these software’s can help to assist
 people at university by helping with time management, organisation, learning concepts, explaining complex information, linking
 information, etc. However, the downsides of this is that it can cause cheating and even accusations of plagiarism for innocent
 people who do not plagiarise on assignments while also raising ethical issues such as using information from other people’s work
 without referencing. It also raises the question of the impact on university students and whether learning for them will be the
 same again as doing work independently develops critical thinking, a skill that is already on the decline today.
